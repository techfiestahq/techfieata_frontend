Our Story

A New Tradition in Nigeria's Tech Ecosystem

Following the remarkable success of past TECHFIESTA editions where professionals gathered to learn, network, and celebrate, we're launching a new tradition: the TECHFIESTA Awards & Dinner Night.

It blends recognition, networking, and storytelling in a relaxed yet intentional settingspotlighting unsung heroes and amplifying the communities that help them rise.

Our Vision

To establish TECHFIESTA as the "Ballon d'Or" of tech impact in Nigeriawhere individuals, grassroots communities, and organizations are recognized for innovation, leadership, and collaboration.

Our Mission

We convene Nigeria's tech ecosystem for an annual, community-curated celebration that:

  • Honors excellence and real-world impact
  • Elevates grassroots communities and their stories
  • Sparks meaningful connections and long-term collaboration
